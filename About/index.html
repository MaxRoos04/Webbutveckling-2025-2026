At Olival Canola Oils, we believe that quality starts at the seed.
Our mission is to deliver premium, heart-healthy canola oil that supports both wellness and culinary excellence.

Sourced from sustainably grown canola fields and refined with care, our oil is rich in omega-3s, low in saturated fat, and ideal for everything from gourmet cooking to everyday meals. Whether you're a home chef or a food industry professional, our cold-pressed and expeller-pressed varieties offer unmatched purity, flavor, and versatility.

We’re proud to serve health-conscious consumers, restaurants, and retailers who value transparency, traceability, and taste. From farm to bottle, we’re committed to sustainability, innovation, and community. Join us in making healthier choices—one drop at a time.
